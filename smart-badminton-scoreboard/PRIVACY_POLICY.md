# Privacy Policy for Smart Badminton Scoreboard

**Last Updated: February 17, 2026**

## Introduction

Smart Badminton Scoreboard ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Player Names**: Names you enter for players in matches
- **Match Data**: Scores, match history, and statistics you create while using the app

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:
- Provide and maintain the app's functionality
- Store your match history and preferences locally on your device
- Process premium purchases

## Data Storage

- **Local Storage Only**: All match data is stored locally on your device
- **No Cloud Sync**: We do not transmit your match data to external servers
- **No Account Required**: The app does not require user registration or accounts

## Third-Party Services

### In-App Purchases
- Processed through Google Play Store or Apple App Store
- Subject to their respective privacy policies
- We do not store payment information

### Advertisements (Free Version)
- May display ads from third-party providers
- Ad providers may automatically collect device identifiers, device type, and operating system version for ad personalization
- You can opt-out of personalized ads in your device settings

## Data Sharing

We do NOT:
- Sell your personal information
- Share your match data with third parties
- Track your location
- Collect personally identifiable information

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: View all data stored by the app
- **Delete**: Clear all match history and data from the app
- **Opt-Out**: Disable personalized advertisements

## Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed for general audiences and does not require age verification.

## Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your data:
- Local storage encryption (device-level)
- No transmission of sensitive data over networks
- Secure in-app purchase processing through platform providers

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted within the app with an updated "Last Updated" date.
